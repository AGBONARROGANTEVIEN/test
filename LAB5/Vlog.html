Major Cities in Japan

  • TokyoThe capital city, famous for Shibuya Crossing and Tokyo Tower.
  • Kyoto Home of traditional temples, geisha districts, and cherry blossoms.
  • Osaka Food capital of Japan, known for takoyaki and Osaka Castle.
  • Hokkaido Winter paradise, skiing, and snow festivals.
  • Hiroshima Historic city with Peace Memorial Park.
  • NaraCity of friendly deer and the giant Buddha.

About Japan

Japan is an island nation in East Asia, blending modern technology with deep traditions. Known as the "Land of the Rising Sun", it is famous for sushi, cherry blossoms, bullet trains, and anime culture.

My Travel Story

I started my vlog in Tokyo, crossing Shibuya and tasting authentic ramen. Then I went to Kyoto to wear a kimono and walk through bamboo forests. In Osaka, I enjoyed takoyaki and Universal Studios. Finally, I visited Mt. Fuji and relaxed in a hot spring (onsen).
