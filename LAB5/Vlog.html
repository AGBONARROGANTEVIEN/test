About Japan

Japan is an island nation in East Asia, blending modern technology with deep traditions. Known as the "Land of the Rising Sun", it is famous for sushi, cherry blossoms, bullet trains, and anime culture.

My Travel Story

My Japan vlog started in Tokyo, where I crossed the busy Shibuya intersection and ate ramen at Ichiran. Then I visited Kyoto, wore a kimono, and strolled through bamboo forests. In Osaka, I tried takoyaki and explored Universal Studios Japan. The highlight was seeing Mt. Fuji and enjoying an onsen (hot spring) in Hakone.
