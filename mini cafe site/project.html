Our Story

Princess Café started as a small dream in 2024, bringing together coffee lovers from all walks of life. With every cup, we want to deliver comfort, joy, and inspiration. Our café is not just about drinks—it's about creating memories.

About Us

Our mini café was founded with a simple mission: to brew happiness. From sourcing locally roasted beans to baking pastries fresh every morning, we focus on quality and heart in everything we do.

Whether you're here to study, chat with friends, or just take a break, Princess Café is your second home.
